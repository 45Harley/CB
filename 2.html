Can Artificial Intelligence help Us?

In a world where democracy teeters on the brink, artificial intelligence emerges as a paradox. Feared for its control, yet brimming with potential to restore transparency and empower citizens.

Imagine "good" AI fact-checking government actions, exposing inefficiencies, and amplifying the people's voice. A "Good AI" rooted in ethics could be the key to rebuilding trust and shaping a resilient democracy. But the question remains: can AI rise above its own limitations to become a force for good?
