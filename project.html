4. Introduction

Goals

  • Modernize local, state, and federal systems to meet today's societal demands.
  • Empower U.S. citizens to actively participate in democratic governance.
  • Ensure secure, transparent, and accountable government operations.

Importance

Rapid technological advancements highlight the need to evolve from outdated systems, fostering trust, efficiency, and individual freedoms.

Outcomes

  • Inclusive and participatory democracy with empowered citizens.
  • Seamless integration of direct democracy with representative governance.
  • Enhanced speed, efficiency, and transparency in governance.
