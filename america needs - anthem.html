“America Needs…”

1. Light as Signal

America needs the light that listens—

Not to shine, but to reveal.

Where truth unfolds in quiet presence,

And justice walks on feet of steel.

2. Heart as Compass

America needs the heart that listens—

Not to feel, but to discern.

Where mercy holds the law in balance,

And every soul has space to turn.

3. Hand as Service

America needs the hand that listens—

Not to grasp, but to extend.

Where labor sings in sacred rhythm,

And work becomes a healing friend.

4. Fire as Purpose

America needs the fire that listens—

Not to burn, but to refine.

Where passion yields to higher order,

And courage walks the holy line.

5. Voice as Governance

America needs the voice that listens—

Not to rule, but to reveal.

Where law unfolds through shared discernment,

And truth becomes the highest seal.

6. Land as Stewardship

America needs the land that listens—

Not to fence, but to befriend.

Where soil is held in sacred keeping,

And dwelling serves the common end.
