Empowering Citizens: The Online Citizens' Branch

Transforming representative democracy into direct democracy.

Concept Features Proposed Reforms Technology

The Concept

The Online Citizens' Branch provides U.S. voters with a platform to engage directly in decision-making processes, making the people the supreme democratic authority.

Key Features

  • Secure validation of voter registration with private accounts.
  • Access to public voting records of elected representatives.
  • Participation in issue-based surveys and voting.
  • Visual representation of historical voting trends.
  • Facilitation of local political group collaboration.

Proposed Reforms

  • Abolish the Electoral College in favor of a national popular vote.
  • Restrict or eliminate presidential pardon powers.
  • Introduce national-level recalls for judges and presidents.
  • Consider alternatives to political parties and traditional leadership models.
  • Impose term limits and ethical standards on Supreme Court justices.
  • Accelerate judicial processes for timely justice.
  • Eliminate gerrymandering and reduce the influence of big money in politics.

The Role of Technology

Leveraging tools like the Google Civic Information API, the platform ensures accessibility, security, and transparency in empowering voters.
